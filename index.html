Journal Article – IEEE

An Exploratory Analysis on Visual Counterfeits Using Conv-LSTM Hybrid Architecture

Mohammad Farukh Hashmi, B. Kiran Kumar Ashish, Avinash G. Keskar, Neeraj Dhanraj Bokde, Jin Hee Yoon, Zong Woo Geem, IEEE Access, vol. 8, pp. 101293-101308, 2020

A deep learning model to detect deep fakes video based on comparing the facial movements of the original with the doctored video of the same subject. Facial landmarks consisting of ears, lips, nose, and eyebrows and their movements are compared and analyzed. The minute differences in the movements are detected and classified into real and deep fake.
